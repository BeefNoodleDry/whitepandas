# WhitePandas — Icebreaker Question Pool

50 questions for the standup icebreaker generator.

---

## Work & Wins

1. What's one personal win and one professional win from last week?
2. What's something you learned recently that surprised you?
3. What's one thing you're proud of shipping or completing lately?
4. What's a challenge you overcame last week — big or small?
5. What's a tool or habit that's made your work life significantly better?
6. What's a skill you've Googled so many times you probably should have just learned it properly by now?
7. What's the best piece of feedback you've ever received — the kind that actually stuck?
8. If you could give yourself one piece of advice on your first day at this company, what would it be?
9. What's a career path you seriously considered before ending up doing what you do now?
10. What's one work habit you've quietly borrowed from a colleague?

## Food & Home

11. If you had to eat only one dish for the rest of your life, what would it be?
12. What was the one purchase you made for your home that changed everything?
13. What's your go-to comfort meal after a rough day?
14. What's the best restaurant you'd take any visitor to in your city?
15. What's the best thing in your fridge right now?
16. What's a food you were convinced you hated as a kid that you actually love now?
17. If you had to describe your cooking confidence as a movie title, what would it be?
18. What's the most recent thing you ordered online that you're genuinely pleased about?
19. What's a dish someone in your family makes that you've never successfully replicated?
20. What's your go-to order when you genuinely cannot decide what you want?

## Fun & Hypotheticals

21. If you could have dinner with anyone — alive or dead — who would it be?
22. If you could live anywhere in the world for a year, where would you go?
23. What's your unpopular opinion about something totally harmless?
24. What's the most useful app on your phone right now?
25. What would you do with an extra hour every day?
26. You wake up tomorrow with one random skill at world-class level. What's the best and worst case for what it could be?
27. If your life had a documentary made about it, what genre would it be and who would direct it?
28. What's something you believed with complete confidence as a child that you now know is wrong?
29. If you had to compete on a TV game show tomorrow, which one gives you the best odds of winning?
30. You're dropped into any historical decade for one weekend — no consequences, you come back after. Which decade, and what's the plan?

## Personal

31. What's something most people don't know about you?
32. What's a skill you've always wanted to learn but haven't yet?
33. What's a hobby you picked up in the last two years?
34. What's the best piece of advice you've ever received?
35. What's something you've been binge-watching or reading lately?
36. What's something you do regularly that most people would be surprised to find out about you?
37. What's a book, film, or show you think is genuinely underrated that almost nobody talks about?
38. Free Saturday, no obligations, no guilt — what does it actually look like? Not the aspirational version, the real one.
39. What's a place you visited with low expectations that genuinely surprised you?
40. What's something you've gotten dramatically better at simply through repetition and time?

## Work Culture

41. What's your ideal work environment — coffee shop, home, office, or something else?
42. Morning person or night owl — and has that changed over time?
43. What's a meeting habit or ritual you wish every team had?
44. What's one thing that makes you feel most productive?
45. What's the best job perk you've ever had?
46. What does your actual desk or workspace look like right now — and does it reflect how you like to work?
47. What's a meeting norm from a previous job — good or bad — that you still think about?
48. When you're in deep focus mode, what's most likely to pull you out of it — and does it actually bother you?
49. What's the smallest, lowest-effort thing a team or manager has done that made you feel genuinely appreciated?
50. Design your perfect work day — not ideal, just genuinely good. What's in it?
